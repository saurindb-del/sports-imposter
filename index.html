How to Play
1
Choose a sport category, difficulty, and number of players. One or two players will secretly be the Imposter.
2
Pass the phone around. Each player taps to see their word. The Imposter sees only the category - not the word!
3
Players take turns describing the word without saying it. Be vague enough that the Imposter can't guess, but clear enough to prove you know it.
4
After discussion, everyone votes on who they think the Imposter is. The Imposter tries to blend in!
5
If the group catches the Imposter, they win! If the Imposter survives, they win!
